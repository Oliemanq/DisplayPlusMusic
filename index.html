Spotify Setup Required

To get started, head to

https://developer.spotify.com/dashboard/

in a browser and sign in. From there, create an app and add a name and description of your choice.
Then add

https://oliemanq.github.io/DisplayPlusMusic/

as a Redirect URI and select 'Web API' at the bottom.
Finally, copy your client ID and client secret from there and paste them below.
